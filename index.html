Western Michigan University Community Health Interest Group

The Western Michigan University Medical School Community Health Interest Group - in partnership with Kalamazoo Gospel Mission, Ministry with Community, and other future partners - provides chronic disease health screening services in the forms of Blood Pressure, Diabetes, and General Health Screenings. Additionally, students are able to bolster their medical education through engagement in thoughtful discussions on nutrition, management of diabetes, hypertension, smoking cessation, and more. Finally, students are able to gain a deeper understanding of the healthcare system and obstacles facing underserved populations.

Sign Up

If you are interested in signing up to help out at an event, you can do so via the signup spreadsheet on Google Docs. Make sure to click on the correct tab at the bottom of the spreadsheet for the event you would like to help out at.

To every event, please bring:

  • Stethoscope
  • WMed Polo OR Scrub Top
  • Face mask

If you want to show up early, someone will be there at least 30 minutes before each event, to start setting up.
